Creating and customizing title cards in Clipchamp

Creating a title card

  1. Click on Open editor. Clipchamp's built-in editing software opens.

  2. Click Text on the left side menu. Editor opens a menu you can scroll through with different text options, including text with different styles.

  3. Select one option and drag it over to the timeline, above where you want it to appear in the video. A textbox appears in the preview and a colored block appears on the timeline.

    Optional: click and drag one edge of the colored block to adjust how long the text appears in the video.

  4. Click inside the text box in the preview.

  5. Type your text in the textbox. Review your video to ensure the title card appears at the right moment and looks the way you want.

Changing the font of the title card

  1. Click on the title from the timeline. The Text property menu opens on the right where you can customize the text.

  2. Click on the dropdown arrow beside the font name. A scrollable dropdown menu opens with different font choices, including headings, subheadings, and body text.

  3. Select a font. The text automatically changes to the selected font.

Adjust the alignment of the text

  1. Open the Text property menu.

  2. Click the alignment icon from the Text property menu.

  3. Select from the three options: Left, Center, or Right. The text within the box follows the alignment you select.

Change the size of the text

  1. Navigate to the preview and click the text.

  2. Click and drag the corners of the text box to adjust its size.

Changing the color of the text

  1. Navigate to the Color section of the text property menu. Depending on the selected text/text style, the default color varies.

  2. Click the colored circle in the menu. Depending on the selected text/text style, the color options offered will vary.

  3. Click a color or type in a HEX value. The text automatically changes
