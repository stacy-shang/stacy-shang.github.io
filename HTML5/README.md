# Who are you and what is this project?

## About me
I am an aspiring technical writer that is interested in user experience and digital accessibility. I am currently taking courses in the Professional Writing and Technical Communication certification program at UMass Amherst.

## About the Accessibility / Flare Project
I am working on this project for an Advanced Technical Writing course within the PWTC program. It aims to inform readers about digital accessibility— its definition, significance, and guidance on best practices to achieve accessibility. This project consists of both my individual research and writing, as well as a team project component.

### The project team
My team’s focus for this project was video transitions. Specifically:
- **Jenna Marino** wrote about key concepts to keep in mind when designing transitions,
- **Mira Kapoor** wrote about best practices for transitions according to WCAG, specifically regarding use of flashing effects and the timing of text slides,
- and I wrote a procedure on how to add and control the timing of transitions on ClipChamp, according to best practices.
