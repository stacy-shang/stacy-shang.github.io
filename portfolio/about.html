About me

Hello! My name is Stacy Shang, and I am a sophomore at the University of Massachusetts Amherst majoring in linguistics with a minor in Spanish. I am pursuing certificates in teaching English as a second language, professional and technical writing, as well as linguistics and data science. In my free time I enjoy going to my local public library, pondering on public transport, finding new music, and solving puzzles.

I am still figuring out my path professionally– whether I want to pursue a career in research, technical writing, or teaching English. I sometimes toy with the idea of getting a Master’s in Library Sciences. But at the core of whatever path I pursue, I hope to use language as a means to share important messages and bridge connections between people.
