About me

Hello! My name is Stacy Shang, and I am a junior Linguistics major with a minor in Spanish at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. I am also in certificate programs for Linguistics & Data Science, Professional Writing and Technical Communication, and Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages. In my free time I enjoy pondering on long walks, going to the library, and trying to code my own blog website

I am pursuing a career in technical writing, with an interest in UX. As a research assistant, I have know far too well the qualms of learning highly-specialized software but finding few helpful resources on how to use it. These experiences have made me personally invested in creating user-friendly documentation that is accessible and usable for everyone.
